Merlot Apartment

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The 120m² apartment is located in Brasília , an iconic city of modernism, built in 1960 by architect Oscar Niemeyer. The building has a concrete structure, a functional and clean plan, with typical modernist elements, such as cobogó and large window frames, with abundant natural light. The property had a lot of potential due to its clean structure, with few pillars and the absence of visible beams, which would give more freedom to create a freer layout. This feature perfectly matched the brief given by the couple, to highlight the modernist character of the building and create an open plan in which the environments are visually connected, with abundant natural light and cross ventilation. As the resident works from home, she wanted an open home office integrated into the social area, with the possibility of acoustic insulation, without giving up natural lighting.

Architects: Studio Clarice Semerene

Area: 120 m²

Year: 2020
